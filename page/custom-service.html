Product introduction:

These perovskite-based nanocrystals offer outstanding photoluminescence and narrow emission linewidths, making them ideal for high-performance optoelectronic applications. Engineered with a CsPbX₃ (X = Cl, Br, I) composition, they exhibit tunable emission wavelengths from 450 nm to 700 nm depending on halide content. The nanoparticles are surface-passivated for enhanced stability and exhibit excellent dispersibility in nonpolar organic solvents such as toluene and hexane. With high quantum yield and size uniformity, they are widely used in research and industry for applications including light-emitting diodes (LEDs), photodetectors, solar cells, and bio-imaging.
