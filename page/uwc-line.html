3.
Product introduction:

This product is a cadmium-based quantum dot substituted with polymer ligands, which is stably dispersed in polar solvents and can be utilized in various bioapplications. The polymer ligands can incorporate various functional groups such as carboxyl, amine, and azide groups, enabling effective covalent bonding with antibodies, proteins, peptides, or other biomolecules. The Uniquedot Water-soluble Cd-based Quantum Dots feature a compact size (below 15 nm) and low nonspecific binding properties, delivering excellent performance in biological environments. They are also specifically designed and optimized for various biological applications, including bioimaging and Lateral Flow Immunoassay (LFA) kits.

4.
Characteristic:
  • Composition: Polymer ligand exchanged CdSe@ZnSeS@ZnS
  • Emission Wavelength: 520 nm ~ 665 nm (color selection available)
  • Quantum Efficiency: ≥ 70%
  • Solvents: Polar solvents such as distilled water, PBS buffer solution, and serum.
5.
Applications:
  • Bioimaging: Precisely targeting cell surface receptors for cancer cell diagnosis and imaging.
  • Lateral Flow Immunoassay (LFA) kits:Used in rapid diagnostics, enhancing sensitivity and reliability.
