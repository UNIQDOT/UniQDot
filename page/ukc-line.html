Product introduction:

Quantum dot solution dispersed in organic solvents. With a core/shell structure, these cadmium-based quantum dots feature excellent color purity and superior dispersibility. They are stably dispersed in organic solvents and are used in various applications. Designed with a CdSe core and CdS-ZnS double shell structure, these quantum dots exhibit emission wavelengths ranging from 520nm to 630nm. They are utilized in various research and industrial applications including photocatalysis, optoelectronic devices, display materials, and optical detection.

  • Solvents: Toluene, hexane, chloroform, and other organic solvents
5.
Applications:

  • Sensor Technology: Sensitive detection and precise analysis
  • Immunochromatography (LFA) Kits: Used in rapid diagnostics with enhanced sensitivity and reliability
