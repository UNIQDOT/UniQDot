Product Name: Water soluble InP-based Quantum dots

Product Code: UWR530 ~ UWR620
3.
Product introduction:

This product is an indium phosphide (InP)-based quantum dot substituted with polymer ligands, which is stably dispersed in polar solvents and can be utilized in a variety of bioapplications. The polymer ligands can incorporate various functional groups such as carboxyl, amine, and azide groups, enabling effective covalent bonding with antibodies, proteins, peptides, or other biomolecules. The Uniquedot Water-soluble InP-based Quantum Dots feature a compact size and low nonspecific binding properties, delivering excellent performance in biological environments. They are also specifically designed and optimized for various biological applications, including bioimaging and Lateral Flow Immunoassay (LFA) kits.

4.
Characteristic:
  • Composition: Polymer ligand exchanged InP@ZnSeS@ZnS
  • Emission Wavelength: 530 nm~620 nm (color selection available)
  • Quantum Efficiency: ≥ 60%
  • Solvents: Polar solvents such as distilled water, PBS buffer solution, and serum.
5.
Applications:
  • Bioimaging: Precisely targeting cell surface receptors for cancer cell diagnosis and imaging.
  • Lateral Flow Immunoassay (LFA) kits:Used in rapid diagnostics, enhancing sensitivity and reliability.
