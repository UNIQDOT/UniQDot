3.
Product introduction:

This product is an InP-based quantum dot with high quality and characteristics, which is stably dispersed in organic solvents and provides excellent optical and electronic properties. The InP core and ZnS double shell structure design allows for the adjustment of emission wavelengths from green to red, ranging from 530 nm to 620 nm. This product is widely used in various research and industrial applications such as photovoltaics, display materials, and optical detection due to its high quantum efficiency, excellent color purity, superior stability, and excellent environmentally friendly characteristics.

4.
Characteristic
  • Composition: InP@ZnSeS@ZnS (core-shell)
  • Emission Wavelength: 530 nm~620 nm (color selection available)
  • Quantum Efficiency: ≥ 90%
  • Solvents: Toluene, hexane, chloroform, and other organic solvents
5.
Applications
  • Optoelectronic Devices: Used in displays and solar cell materials
  • Sensor Technology: High-sensitivity LiDAR sensors and precise analysis
  • Immunochromatography (LFA) Kits: Used in rapid diagnostics with enhanced sensitivity and reliability
